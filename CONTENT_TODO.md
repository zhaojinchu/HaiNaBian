# Content required before launch

## Teacher identity and credibility

- [ ] Full Chinese name and preferred English name
- [ ] Approved biography in Chinese and English
- [ ] Teaching qualifications, exact issuing organizations, and dates
- [ ] Teaching experience described without inflated claims
- [ ] Languages spoken and level used for teaching or parent communication
- [ ] Experience with overseas Chinese learners
- [ ] Experience teaching children and teenagers
- [ ] Approved teacher portrait
- [ ] Approved teaching-environment and lesson photographs with usage rights

## Lessons and logistics

- [ ] Accepted student ages and levels
- [ ] Lesson durations
- [ ] Online teaching platform
- [ ] Confirm lessons at the teacher’s home and whether the location may be published
- [ ] Available days and hours
- [ ] Materials used, supplied, or charged separately
- [ ] Confirm one-to-one, two-to-one, and groups of three to four

## Pricing and policies

- [ ] Final prices from the teacher
- [ ] Payment currency
- [ ] Package sizes and validity
- [ ] Payment methods
- [ ] Cancellation, late arrival, no-show, and rescheduling policy
- [ ] Refund policy
- [ ] Parent-observation policy
- [ ] Privacy notice and consent wording

## Contact and integrations

- [ ] Public contact email
- [ ] Public phone or WhatsApp number
- [ ] WeChat ID or QR code
- [ ] Preferred contact channels and expected response window

## Editorial approval

- [ ] Final Chinese and English page copy
- [ ] Final slogan or English brand descriptor
- [ ] Approved explanation of the name 海那边
- [ ] Real testimonials with written publication permission
- [ ] Domain name and canonical site URL
- [ ] Social sharing image and favicon approval
